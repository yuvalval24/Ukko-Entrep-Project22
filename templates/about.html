There are 3 different types of companies in this market:
- Immunotherapy to help people change their immunity level over time, creating medicine and drugs to cure them one way or another. For example taking a pill before specific food. These companies have not been successful and have not passed clinical trials.
- Some companies have worked to develop wheat with reduced toxicity of the gluten (agrotech companies/academia). This wheat would have lower levels of gluten in order to reduce how bad the gluten sensitivity is. However, the issue is that the companies cannot reduce the gluten completely in the plant. Without proteins (gluten) in the plant, the plant cannot live. Without this, there will never be a perfectly safe product for people with Celiac.
- Gluten-free products are the last option. However, these products are not able to have the same properties (texture, etc.) as versions with gluten. Additives like sugars, salts, etc. are added in order to try to compensate, but they may end up creating products with a worse nutritional value.
